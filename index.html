A Note From Us

Why we built this

We've spent years working with local business owners — restaurateurs, dentists, plumbers, salon owners — who were pouring their heart into their craft while losing customers to less-qualified competitors who simply showed up better online.

The problem wasn't their business. It was their visibility. Wrong addresses on Google. No photos. Old websites that broke on phones. Reviews going unread and unanswered. The tools to fix this existed, but they required either deep technical knowledge or a dedicated marketing hire — neither of which most local business owners have.

ShowUpLocal exists to close that gap. We combine smart AI tools with real human expertise to make sure your business shows up first when the customers you're meant to serve go looking for you.
